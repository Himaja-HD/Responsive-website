About Us

Promoting Sustainable Livelihood And Self-Reliance Among Tribal People Community.

Our Goushala is inspiring and encouraging the tribal community of India
to grow and develop. We believe that they must make a sustainable living
for self-reliance and increased confidence. Therefore, we are passionate
about enlightening and enriching the tribal community with their skills and
craftsmanship. With a mission to bridge the gap between the tribal and
modern civilization, Our Goushala is supporting tribal women, men and farmers to
“Earn a pay better than fair” with the help of their natural produce
from the land and livestock.
